My Research

A Progressive Step Towards Automated Fact-Checking by Detecting Context in Diverse Languages: A Prototype for Bangla Facebook Posts

Developed an NLP-based "Automated Context Detector" to enhance fact-checking for Bangla Facebook posts, achieving promising results. Built a custom dataset and evaluated both traditional ML models and advanced NLP models, showcasing the potential of ML in fact-checking and providing a framework for similar applications.

Status: Under Review at ENASE 2025

Streamlining Facebook Fact-Checking: The Role of Proposed Automated Tools

This study explores the spread of false information on Facebook in Bangladesh and identifies gaps in current fact-checking practices through surveys and expert interviews. It proposes automated tools like opinion detectors, context detectors, and information extractors to enhance efficiency, with potential applications in similar cultural contexts globally.

Status: Expected submission, January 2025

Target Journal: PAJAIS

Leveraging LLMs to Extract Gender Profiles from Social Media posts in the Low-Resource Language Bangla

This research focuses on gender profiling in Bangla social media posts, leveraging writing styles to identify anonymous authors. By utilizing large language models (LLMs), it addresses the unique challenges of Bangla as a low-resource language, enhancing cybersecurity and combating identity theft.

Status: Ongoing Project
